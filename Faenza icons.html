faenza-icons

An Anki2 add-on

Replace the sync and statistics icons.

This is an abandoned effort to give Anki 2 a more colorful look. If you liked the color icons of Anki 1, you may take a look at the colorful toolbar add-on.

The icons are taken from the KFaenza KDE icon theme.
