More shortcuts

An Anki2 add-on

Reactivate two Anki 1 shortcuts:

  • Ctrl+f card browser
  • Ctrl+w Deck overview

During reviews, the audio is replayed with the “6” or “i” keys. Those are convenient when using the numpad or a Dvorak keyboard with the Dvorak keys add-on.

This add-on is based on oldshortcuts.py, the ... Anki 1.2 shortcuts.... add-on, but it drops Ctr+d for add card.
