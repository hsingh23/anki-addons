Anki 1.2 shortcuts

An Anki2 add-on

Reactivate two Anki 1 shortcuts:

  • Ctrl+f card browser
  • Ctrl+w Deck overview

This is just a little rework of the oldshortcuts.py add-on, dropping Ctr+d for add card.

The main point of this add-on was to bring back F5 for replay-audio. This is now again a standard shortcut in Anki 2, so the point of this add-on is basically gone. You may as well use the original.
